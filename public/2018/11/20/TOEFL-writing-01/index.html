    1.1 Do you agree or disagree with the following statement: All the university students should take history courses no matter what field they major in.

    Agree


    1. foster one’s rational thinking and critical thought 培养理性思维和批判思维

    2. get a clear perspective of oneself 了解自己

    3. make sound judgment on 对于……做出理性的判断

    4. enhance one’s literacy 增强一个人的读写能力

    5. avoid making similar mistakes 避免犯相似的错误

    6. enlarge one’s scope of knowledge 增加知识面

    7. broaden one’s mental horizons 拓宽视野

    8. expand one’s mind 开拓视野(口语推荐)

    9. learn the relevant experiences from the historical events 从历史事件中学习相关经验

    10. take history as a mirror 以史为鉴

    11. mature one’s thought 成熟思想

    12. successful figures 成功的人

    13. mirroring the experiences of failure 学习失败教训

    14. The difficulty is the best helper for steeling will 困难是磨练意志最好的帮手


    Disagree


    1. if one’s major is totally irrelevant to history 如果一个人的专业和历史完全无关

      解析：such as botany or zoology 例如动物学和动物性


    2. to learn history will distract one’s energy and waster one’s time学习历史会分散精力，浪费时间

    3. have nothing to do with our current life 和我们当前的生活联系不大


    Opening Paragraph


    1. There is no denying that …


    state point


    1. this view doesn’t hold water 这一观点是站不住脚的

    2. Reasonable as it may sound, I do not think this view can hold the water 听起来很合理，但我认为这种观点不可能成立

    3. I agree with this idea for a few reasons.

    4. It is because of these that you will be better at English

    5. This virtually to the student’s quality put forward higher requirements

    6. strengthens 加强

    7. cultural deposits 文化底蕴

    8. committing blunders 犯错误

    9. sound judgment 正确的判断

    10. absorb experience

    11. by means of 依靠

    12. take a keen interest in 对…有浓厚的兴趣

    13. reading extensively 广泛阅读

    14. conduces to 有助于

    15. everyone has an obligation to

    16. put more academic pressures


    Model Essay

    University courses lay a very solid foundation for the future development of college students. Facing the great selections of these courses, some people say that history classes should be made compulsory for college students, for the reason that they could be more familiar with the advancement and evolution of human society. I disagree. I believe, despite minor merits, asking student of all major to attend history classes would cause problems.


    My imitation

    There is no denying that selecting courses is very important to college students, which affect students’ future development a lot. Some people say that history courses should be taken by all college students, I agree with this idea for a few reasons.


    The main reason that I approve of this view is that learning history could enlarge students’ scope of knowledge, which help students foster their rational and critical thought. For example, an engineering student may never know about the birth of his major if he doesn’t learn history. It is because of this irrelevant history knowledge that we mature our thought. Moreover, students have put forward higher requirements in today’s world. They are supposed to be more well-rounded, therefore, learning curricula, such as history, is essential.


    In general, having a good knowledge of history not only strengthens students’ cultural deposits, but also helps them learn to take history as a mirror, which could help students absorb the relevant experiences from the historical events to avoid committing similar blunders and make sound judgment. Many successful people attribute their success to the habit of reading history, they take a keen interest in studying history and reading extensively, which contribute to their later achievements in personal career. In other words, they benefit from the past histories rely on learning successful figures and mirroring the experiences of failure. According to them, we could find that studying history indeed facilitate our adolescent no matter what field we major in.


    In addition, traditional culture has become a gene of our nation, learning history is an important way to help our national cultural heritage. As a citizen, everyone has an obligation to passing on our culture. The long history finally breeds our glorious culture over thousands of years, which subtly influenced our thinking and behavior, and root in our inside deeply. History witness all the progress and evolutions, nothing could be better than learning history to inherit our culture.


    Admittedly, learning history does have some drawbacks. On one side, students must spend more time in history courses, which means they have less time on their major, it would put more academic pressures on students. On the other hand, for some students who doesn’t like history, the compulsory history class is a torture, which may influence the score seriously.


    In conclusion, although there are some drawbacks of compulsion of history class, making every college student take history course would bring them more possibilities in the future. Besides, those difficulties would be the best helper for steeling will.
